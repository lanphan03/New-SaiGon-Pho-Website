About Us

Hudson Valley's
taste of Saigon.

New SaiGon Pho was created from a simple idea: to bring the comfort, flavor, and warmth of Vietnamese cooking to the heart of Newburgh.

Inspired by the busy streets of Saigon, where the smell of simmering broth, grilled meats, fresh herbs, and Vietnamese coffee fills the air, our restaurant brings those memories to the Hudson Valley. Every bowl of pho starts with a rich, slow-cooked beef broth made with care. Every banh mi is grilled, crisp, and full of flavor. Every vermicelli bowl, rice plate, and drink is made to feel fresh, comforting, and satisfying.

For us, Vietnamese food is more than a meal. It is family, tradition, and hospitality. It is the bowl of soup that warms you on a cold day, the fresh herbs that brighten every bite, and the familiar taste that makes you feel at home.

At New SaiGon Pho, we are proud to share the taste of Saigon with our Newburgh community. Whether you come in for pho, banh mi, bubble tea, Vietnamese coffee, or a quick meal with family and friends, we want every visit to feel welcoming, fresh, and memorable.
